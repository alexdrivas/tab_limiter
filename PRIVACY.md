# Privacy Policy for Tab Limiter

**Last Updated: November 17, 2025**

## Overview
Tab Limiter is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection
Tab Limiter **does not collect any user data**. The extension:
- Does not track your browsing history
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not transmit any data to external servers

## Data Storage
The extension stores the following preferences **locally on your device only**:
- Maximum tab limit setting (number)
- Enabled/disabled state (boolean)
- Behavior mode preference (block/warn/auto-close)

This data is stored using Chrome's `chrome.storage.sync` API and may sync across your Chrome browsers if you have Chrome Sync enabled. This data never leaves Google's infrastructure and is not accessible to the extension developer.

## Permissions Used
- **tabs**: To count and manage your open tabs
- **storage**: To save your preferences locally
- **notifications**: To alert you when you reach your tab limit

## Third-Party Services
Tab Limiter does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

## Changes to This Policy
Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted in this document and updated in the Chrome Web Store listing.
